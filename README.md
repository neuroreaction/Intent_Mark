# Intent_Mark
Intent Mark Privacy Policy
App: IntentMark
Developer: David Phillips (CellphoneProductions)
Effective Date: 2/2/2026

Privacy Summary

IntentMark is designed to keep your data on your device. The app does not require an account and does not send your entries to the developer.

Data We Collect

None. IntentMark does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. This includes identifiers, contact information, location, usage activity, or any data you enter into the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

Information you create or configure in IntentMark (for example: logs, counters, history, and settings) is stored locally on your device for the purpose of providing the app’s functionality.

Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

IntentMark does not use advertising and does not include third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. The app does not track you across apps or websites.

Permissions

IntentMark does not request access to device permissions such as Contacts, Location, Photos, Camera, Microphone, Network, Bluetooth or Health data.

Backups

If you back up your device (iCloud or computer backup), app data may be included in that backup depending on your device settings. The developer does not receive your backup data.

Data Removal

Deleting the app typically removes locally stored app data from the device.

Children’s Privacy

IntentMark does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.
